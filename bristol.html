Accommodation

I can still remember before I came to the UK. I was excited but worried. I was excited because I would go to a new place and meet new people, especially so many foreigners for me. But I was nervous because my English is not very good. I might not be able to express myself very well, and the thing would be worse if I was in trouble and needed some help.

As I was worried about, an embarrassing thing happened to me when I went to the accommodation and registered. A pretty lady helped to register and gave me the key, but I had to wait until another stuff is available to help me because I have no idea about where my room is. A few minutes later, another pretty lady came to me and asked me some questions, but unfortunately, I could not understand even one word. So, I said “sorry?” with a confused emotion. The pretty might be aware of something, and she spoke again patiently and slowly. Though I can understand more than the first time, I still cannot fully understand her, so I showed my confusing emotion to her again. At the time, the lady who had helped me register helped me answer the question. Then we did not talk anymore during walking to my room except “thank you!” and “you are welcome.”

Apart from the embarrassing thing, I found an interesting phenomenon. That is the room number is as same as that of where I had lived in during my undergraduate; they are both number 308.

what an impressive experience!
