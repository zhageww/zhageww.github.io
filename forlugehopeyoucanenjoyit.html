Study

Studying in UK might be a new experience for many students, they may feel excited, curious but worried. In the new environment, fresh students may know different thing from their existing knowledge, make friends with various people even from lots of countries, however, there are still some challenges they need to overcome. Here I would like to share some helpful information with you! Hope it can help!

Language barrier:

The first thing that you find trick to deal with when you take off the plane is the language. You speak Mandarin, but people living UK speak English, which cause some language barrier for you. However, there is no need to warry about it, no one is a genius when they were born after all. There are some tips for you to improve you English: 

  • Be confident: When you talk with the native speakers, do not construct a constriction for yourselves, just be confident. For example, you can ignore all the grammar rules, tenses and even gender (since so many Chinese students may find it difficult to distinguish “she” and “he”). The main purpose of you is to express yourself. Therefore, when you meet so words that you do not know how to express, you can express it as some phrases or even a sentence instead.
